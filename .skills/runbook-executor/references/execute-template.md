# Runbook Execution Record Template

Use this structure to fill `## 执行记录` while keeping the plan step titles unchanged.

```md
## 执行记录

### 步骤 1 - <step title from plan>
#### 执行
- Agent: <@吕布 | @张辽 | other selected general>
- Actions:
- Commands:
- Files changed:
- Evidence:

#### 验收
- Agent: <@吕布 | @荀彧 | other selected official>
- Checks:
- Commands:
- Result:
- Evidence:

### 步骤 2 - <step title from plan>
#### 执行
- Agent:
- Actions:
- Commands:
- Files changed:
- Evidence:

#### 验收
- Agent:
- Checks:
- Commands:
- Result:
- Evidence:
```

Rules:
- Do not rename steps.
- Do not omit either subsection.
- Do not replace evidence with a vague success claim.
